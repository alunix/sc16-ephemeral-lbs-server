Welcome to the help page!

Here you will get some help and hints for using the website!

The website offers you several possibilities to explore activities in your city. Where is the best party? Where is the cheapest place to eat or where are the nicest places in the city? On the left side you can see a map with all existing zones. One the right side you will later see the messages, statistics or set options for your own zone. To start you can either choose an existing zone in the map, search for a zone in the search bar or create a new zone.

Searching zones

To find a zone you can use the search functionality. You can search for a zone with its name or by topics. The search then will provide a list with all relevant zones, e.g the keyword "BBQ" searches for zones with this keyword in their name as well as for zones that have this keyword as a topic.


Accessing messages

To access the messages that were shared within a zone, you have to click on the zone. I there are overlapping zones you have to choose one from the appearing popup. After choosing one the messages are displayed in the message window on the right side. They are ordered from the newest to the oldest message. You can also search for messages of a specific topic, e.g the keyword "BBQ" searches for messages that were shared under the topic "BBQ".


Creating zones

For creating a zone you have to use the button. After you have clicked on it the button will disappear and the edit modus will open.


Now you can select the drawing tool and draw your zone directly on the map. When you have finished the zone polygon, it will disappear on the map and its coordinates are added into the form. Now you have to set the zone properties. It is mandatory to set a name, a duration until when the zone should exists and at least one topic for the zone. You can add several topics for one zone. The duration can be defined with the poping up calender in the corresponding field. In the calender you can choose a date and a time until when the zone should exist.


The coordinates are already inserted automatically if you have finished drawing the zone. With the button you can now submit the new zone and finish its creation. There is also the possibility to reset the current entries.

Exploring statistics

The website offers statistics that show the activity in each zone. For exploring the statistics you have to choose a zone in the map, the statistic will then be generated automatically.


Now you know how the website works. Have fun and explore it!
